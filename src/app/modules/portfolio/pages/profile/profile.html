Khondaker Abu Ashfaque

Khondaker Abu Ashfaque is a Bangladeshi political leader and a senior figure of the Bangladesh Nationalist Party (BNP). He currently serves as the President of Dhaka District BNP, playing an important role in organizing and leading party activities at the district level.

Political Role

As President of Dhaka District BNP, Khondaker Abu Ashfaque is responsible for party coordination, grassroots leadership, and strengthening BNP’s organizational structure across the district. He is regarded as an influential leader within the party due to his active involvement in political movements and organizational work.

Parliamentary Candidacy

Khondaker Abu Ashfaque has been nominated by BNP as the parliamentary candidate for the Dhaka-1 constituency. The Dhaka-1 seat includes the Dohar and Nawabganj areas, where he represents the party’s political vision and commitment to public service.

Public Engagement

He maintains regular contact with party leaders, activists, and supporters. Through meetings, public gatherings, and political programs, he works to promote BNP’s policies and connect with the local community.

Vision and Political Aim

Khondaker Abu Ashfaque envisions a democratic Bangladesh built on justice, accountability, and equal rights for all citizens. His political aim is to strengthen democratic values and ensure that people’s voices are respected in national decision-making.

As President of Dhaka District BNP, he works to organize and energize grassroots leadership. He believes strong local leadership is the foundation of a strong and democratic state.

As a BNP-nominated parliamentary candidate for Dhaka-1, he is committed to serving the people of Dohar and Nawabganj. His goal is to address public concerns through honest representation, fair governance, and people-centered development.

He strongly supports youth empowerment, employment opportunities, and social justice. His political vision focuses on restoring public trust, protecting democratic institutions, and working for a future where governance serves the people, not power.
